MIYAZAKI, FUKUOKA

Dummy text model course in Kyushu

*取材期間：2017年7月19日～23日

1DAY (Fukuoka, Oita)

1:20
Departure from Changi Airport
08:20

Arrival to Fukuoka Airport
12:00
Local Cuisine "Yufumabushi Shin"

For the specialty dish, "kama-meshi" rice, you can choose one out of three kinds of toppings: Bungo-gyu beef, chicken, or eel, all produced in Oita Prefecture. You can enjoy your choice of topping on rice with condiments and pickles or with dashi soup, to create your own preferred taste.

Local Cuisine "Yufumabushi Shin"
14:00
Traditional Japanese Rickshaw

You can enjoy peaceful streets, rural landscapes, temples and shrines in the mountains in Yufuin from a rickshaw, which gives different views from when you are walking, bicycling, or riding in a car.

Traditional Japanese Rickshaw
15:00
Yunotsubo Kaido Street

As one of the most popular areas for strolling in Yufuin, this street is lined with about 70 shops and stores, including restaurants, craft shops, and sweets shops.

Yunotsubo Kaido Street
16:00
Yufuin Hot Springs

This onsen resort attracts people with its abundant spring water and splendid view from the open-air baths. The water is smooth and conducive to beautiful skin as well as recovery from fatigue. Enjoy onsen with various positive effects.

Yufuin Hot Springs

2DAY(Kumamoto)

8:00
Departure from the hotel
9:00
Kokonoe "Yume" Grand Suspension Bridge

Located 173 meters above the river, this 390-meter hanging bridge has the highest altitude of all pedestrian-only bridges in Japan. Stunning views below the bridge indeed make it a "walkway in the sky."

Kokonoe "Yume" Grand Suspension Bridge
10:45
Kuju Flower Park

Located in a national park, this flower park is a perfect place to enjoy beautiful nature to the fullest. The vast park is adorned with 5 million flowers of 500 kinds throughout the year.

Kuju Flower Park
12:15
Local Cuisine "Hanabishi"

This restaurant offers local dishes with local ingredients and pure water of the Aso area using traditional cooking methods. The popular dish "gyu-katsu ju" is a rice dish topped with a cutlet made from Kumamoto’s brand beef, Akaushi, which is cooked rare and served with wasabi and soy sauce.

Local Cuisine "Hanabishi"
14:00
Helicopter Tour in Aso

To fully enjoy the vast nature of Mount Aso, you should take a helicopter ride. Enjoy a panoramic view of the splendid Kusasenri prairie with a beautiful contrast between green grass and blue water from midair.

Helicopter Tour in Aso
16:00
Shirakawa Hot Spring

This "hidden hot spring" in the interior of the Aso area is located south of Kurokawa Onsen, which is one of the most popular onsen areas in Japan. This area features luxury Japanese-style hotels surrounded by abundant nature.

Shirakawa Hot Spring

3DAY(Miyazaki)

8:30
Departure from the hotel
10:45
Takachiho Gorge

As one of the most popular tourist spots in Kyushu, this gorge with steep cliffs with a height of 80 meters on average and stretching east and west for about seven kilometers has been designated a Natural Monument and National Place of Scenic Beauty. The area has well-maintained pathways and there is also a rental boat service.

Takachiho Gorge
12:00
Somen Nagashi "Chiho-no-ie"

This restaurant at Takachiho Gorge invented "nagashi somen," a style of serving somen noodles where thin somen noodles are fed on a stream through a down-slanted bamboo flume to hungry guests waiting at the side of the flume. In addition to the famous nagashi somen, you can also enjoy delicious snacks, including charcoal-grilled "yamame" trout and "dango mochi" balls.

Somen Nagashi "Chiho-no-ie"
15:45
Horikiri Pass & Roadside Station "Phoenix"

This roadside station at Nichinan Phoenix Road has a popular observation deck from which you can have a panoramic view of the Pacific Ocean. Enjoy soft ice cream made with Miyazaki mangoes and "hyuga-natsu" oranges.

Horikiri Pass & Roadside Station "Phoenix"
18:30
Seafood "Amimoto

Take a seat in one of the private rooms looking down at the large fish tank at the center of the restaurant. You can enjoy fresh seafood along with dishes made with locally-produced chicken, including Miyazaki’s specialty "chicken-namban."

Seafood "Amimoto

4DAY (Miyazaki, Kagoshima, Fukuoka)

8:30
Departure from the hotel
8:45
Aoshima Shrine

Located at almost the center of Aoshima Island, which has been worshipped as a holy area since ancient times, this shrine is a famous spot for marriage. Offering prayers here will bring you romance.

Aoshima Shrine
9:45
Nichinan Phoenix Road

A perfect driving route to have a panoramic view of the coast, which has many spots to enjoy wonderful views along with popular sightseeing places along the way.

Nichinan Phoenix Road
10:30
Udo Shrine

Uniquely standing inside the cave facing the ocean on the beach, this bright red painted shrine has a unique way of wishing for luck: if you toss an "undama," a small round piece of unglazed clay, and it goes into the hole on the back of a turtle-shaped rock, your wish will be fulfilled!

Udo Shrine
12:00
Local Cuisine "Gallery Kodama"

Located in a renovated Japanese-style house built more than 100 years ago, this restaurant has a nice atmosphere. "Katsuo-aburi ju," rice topped with charcoal-grilled marinated bonito, is a popular dish.

Local Cuisine "Gallery Kodama"
15:59
Departure from Kagoshima-Chuo Station
17:16
Arrival to Hakata Station
18:30
Food stalls "Nakasu-Yatai"

At night, street stalls are set up one after another, creating an area said to have the highest number of street stalls in Japan. Eat Hakata’s local dishes and have a drink with local people, and you will have an unforgettable memory of your trip.

Food stalls "Nakasu-Yatai"
