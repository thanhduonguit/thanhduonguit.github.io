Access from Singapore

day. It takes 6 hours 15 minutes from Singapore to Fukuoka by direct flight and 6 hours and 25 minutes from Fukuoka to Singapore.The airport is directly connected with subway lines, taking you to central Fukuoka in just 5 minutes. You can get to the downtown shopping area in about 10 minutes.

Access in the Kyushu

Kyushu Shinkansen
In recent years, access to each prefecture became very convenient as the Kyushu Shinkansen which gathered the best technologies in Japan opened. The Shinkansen running through Kyushu at high speed has the best ride comfort. Seats with traditional fabric fabrics and guest room colored with traditional crafts will create a relaxing journey. Please also check the excellent JR Kyushu rail pass.

Kyushu Rail Pass (JR Kyushu) http://www.jrkyushu.co.jp/english/railpass/railpass.html
